Every journey has their own starting line. What's mine?

My own journey started when I was introduced to gaming with the old PSP 3000 at 4 years old. I saw it with my very own eyes, touched it with my very own hands. It gave me the sudden interest and as time passed by; it absolutely became my hobby, let’s call it my PASSION.

What is passion in gaming for someone like me?

In general, passion is a strong liking or desire. But we all have our own interpretation and understanding of different words, am I right? Personally, passion means something in my ability that keeps me going, sparks up my excitement and happiness whenever I encounter nor do a certain thing related to something I love. Passion is the personal thing and interest that I enjoy, good or bad, as long as I’m determined to be better at it.

How to actually enjoy Gaming?

WHY I GAME

1. Gaming gives you a perspective of ideas and fantasies

Gaming can create limitless opportunities that can put you in different unique scenarios that involves the use of brainpower such as puzzles, escapes, battle strategies, etc. These can help you learn to create the best strategies in similar situations.

2. Gaming can boost certain physical abilities

By training the brain to to use eyes effectively in games, it can increase reaction time and improve vision. It can also help strengthen hand muscles due to frequent use.

3. Games can improve social skills and confidence

Multiplayer games can give you a headstart in being more sociable. Interaction with other players in the game can give you the experience and confidence to be able to socialize more while being able to cooperate better due to the collaborative components of some games. You are also most likely to make friends ingame or in real life due to similar likings to a specific video game topic and interests.

4. Games can help you become more motivated

Games as my passion are able to motivate me do my works. They act as a gift to myself when I am able to finish doing something important. This motivation can push me to do my work efficiently in order to be able to play video games soon after.
