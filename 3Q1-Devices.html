The Playstation Portable

The Playstation Portable is the first ever gaming device I used with my own hands. As a pre-elementary student, it didn't really interest me at first. I only use it when I feel like using it. However, as I keep playing it as time pass by, I started to play it more often; opening its system most of the times when I'm free and playing my games. It became my ultimate hobby and passion for the next several years until a better generation of gaming devices took over- The Playstation Consoles.

I never owned a Playstation 3, but I did get to use it a lot back then around 2014. My uncle originally owned it but he lets us borrow his Console for an extended period of time. My little brother and I always used to play the old Need for Speed and Gran Turismo 5 on it. We tend to fight over who holds the controller as we only have one to play with.
